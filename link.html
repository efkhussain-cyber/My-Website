RMS

RMS stands for Request Management System. It is a software application that helps organizations manage and track requests from customers, employees, or other stakeholders. The system allows users to submit requests, track their progress, and receive updates on their status. RMS can be used for various types of requests, such as IT support, maintenance, or service requests. It helps improve efficiency and communication within an organization by providing a centralized platform for managing requests.
